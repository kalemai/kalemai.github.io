Organisation profile:

Community Health Initiatives-Uganda is a Community Based organization established in 2019 and registered in 2021 by a group of volunteers after realizing the need to address health barriers to community development to enhance community cooperation for improved health and well-being conditions. It is registered by the district local government under registration number MYG/DNGOMC/011 with the board of directors that comprises of 5 members and operating its own bank account.

Principle Activities:

  • HIV/Aids Awareness, prevention and education.
  • Sexual and reproductive health and rights.
  • Community care and support as well as community development.
  • Health education and promotions
  • Menstrual Hygiene.
  • Sanitation and Water, Sanitation and Hygiene.

Our Service detailed

As listed above, hear we give more hights on each of the services offer.

HIV/AIDS Awareness, Prevention, and Education

This activity involves sensitizing communities about HIV/AIDS transmission, prevention methods, and the importance of early testing and treatment. It promotes safe practices, combats stigma and discrimination, and empowers individuals with knowledge to make informed health decisions.

Sexual and Reproductive Health and Rights (SRHR)

SRHR ensures individuals have access to information and services related to sexuality, family planning, and reproductive choices. This includes promoting rights to safe pregnancy, access to contraceptives, prevention of sexually transmitted infections, and respect for bodily autonomy, especially for women and youth.

Community Care and Support as well as Community Development

This activity focuses on strengthening local support systems for vulnerable groups, including people living with HIV, orphans, and the elderly. It also involves empowering communities through capacity building, income-generating activities, and sustainable development initiatives that improve overall well-being.

Health Education and Promotions

Health education and promotion aim to increase public awareness about healthy lifestyles, disease prevention, and health services. Through campaigns, workshops, and media, it encourages behavior change, improved nutrition, vaccination uptake, and regular medical check-ups.

Vulnerable communities

Include Children, youths, Adolescent girls and young women, pregnant mothers, elderly people, malnourished people and people who are I'll or Immune compromised.

Disadvantaged Communities

Include people suffering from a combination of economic, health and environmental burdens which includes poverty, high levels of unemployment, air and water pollution, emergencies etc.

Real estate business tips

Make sure that you hire the right real estate agent

Real estate professionals agree that the best investment that a seller can make is to spend time researching the proper agent for their property. This is because a good agent can save you thousands of dollars by ensuring a quick turnaround of your house after you put it on the market. The first step to picking the right agent is to analyze out their online promotional material for quality. Secondly, have a look at their profiles on social media and gauge their market appeal. Proceed to create a shortlist and interact on a first-hand basis with each agent. While doing so, ask about their current listings, reports of sales-activity, average time on the market for their listings and local competition. A good real estate agent understands the best time to organize open houses and initiate price wars to their client’s benefit.
