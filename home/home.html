Background

Glym Chemicals Ltd is a food grade chemical processing venture operating in Uganda. This enterprise came up in 2014 as a result of very many challenges which included:-
no locally made food grade chemicals, high cost imports of baking powder, unreliable food grade chemicals supply chain, hard to find good starch and food derivatives, inaccessibility and limited distribution, differential prices, scattered and little information about chemicals and food grade chemicals, price hiking due increased import costs, poor customer engagement to mention but a few. The growing per capital consumption trends of bakery products which include cake, pastries, biscuits, bread, breakfast cereals and other products indicated untapped growth potential in the global bakery industry as the report by Research and Market Agency indicated that market size of bakery products and food grade chemicals is expected to reach USD 530 billion by 2021. Uganda has a population of 36 million people and its economy is estimated to grow at over 6% per annum for the next 5 years with the demand of starch and bread related products expected to explode in the coming years attributed to the growing population and middle class income earners. Whereas the general economic climate seems to be depressed, the food and chemical sector remains vibrant. .

With increased use of locally available raw material, cheap manufacturing inputs, responsive supply chains and use of high tech will solve the above challenges with lower cost of marketing and production, larger markets made available due to global reach, increased customer engagement, better content, accessible and reliable supply chain will enable business activity. Glym Chemicals Ltd is a limited by share company which is a quality based chemical and food grade chemicals manufacturing facility owned by Bakasambe Robert holding 70% shares, Matovu Kato Davis holding 20%, and Najjuko Noeline with 10% shares. Glym Chemicals Ltd is a full-service manufacturing facility dedicated to consistently providing high quality food grade chemicals products such as Glucose, cornstarch, dextrin, glucose, syrup and baking soda at an acceptable price/value relationship and with a responsive supply chain. We maintain a friendly, fair, and creative work environment, which respects diversity, ideas, and hard work. With increased technology and smart phones, social media, and the internet, digital data will be used for online engagements like E- ordering, monitoring of demand and supply thus solving the above challenges with lower cost of marketing and production, larger markets made available due to global reach, increased customer engagement, better content with a very responsive supply chain
